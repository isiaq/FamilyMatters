About Us

Bimbo Family Affairs is a platform created for all matters relating to the family. We see the family as the foundation of our existence and as such, we need one another for nourishment and growth. We believe that if each of us can take responsibility for our family, society will be better for us and as such; everyone in this is an integral part of the unit and must be respected. This initially started as a family blog on matters relating to relationships, parenting, children and general life style, our goal is to restore the society one family at a time. Through our interactive sessions, counselling, writings, public speaking curriculum development and other possible ways, we want our message of love and a unified family to reach the world.

Abimbola Somolu Profile
Abimbola has over the years developed a voice through her blog: bimbofamilyaffairs and public speaking for the advocacy of the family unit in which good parenting and well-grounded children are essential. She believes that if everyone can take responsibility for their family, the society will be the better for it, hence the ‘Agbajowo’ series. The Yorubas’ are known for their wise and pithy sayings, one of which says “Agbajowo ni a fi soya” (you cannot thump your chest in pride with just one of your fingers, you need all of your fingers to do that). We need a sense of ‘collective responsibility’ to raise children we can be proud of, ‘our collective hands –Agbajowo’ coming together to fulfill the noble task of raising our children as; Family is the bed-rock of the Society.
