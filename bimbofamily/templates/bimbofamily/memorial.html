Memorial

In memory of my strong, bold, principled, assertive, selfless, loyal and resourceful mother, Comfort Adunni Jemibewon (C.A.J) (The Warrior) who through her personal struggles and life battles still fought and did everything within her to keep our family together against all odds. An encourager, defender, giver, organiser and fighter for a good cause (she loathed people being cheated) She made us realise that Our varying qualities and characteristics were to be used to the advantage of the family. She imbibed in us strong values and taught us to treat people with respect, stand up for what is right, stretched and pushed us to be responsible, compassionate, humble and accommodating. An indivisible unit with strong family values, she trained us to never leave anyone behind but she to use our strengths to cover each other's weakness. With a lot of stories to tell, she always reminded us that the blessing of one is the blessing of all and that wealth is only valuable when it is used to bring those around you along the way to prosperity without making any fuss about it. These were what I lived by and want to pass on.
